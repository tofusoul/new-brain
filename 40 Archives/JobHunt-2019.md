4.  Job Description

    **Financial Services - Te Ratonga Ahumoni**

    -   Full-time at 37.5 hours per week (1.0 FTE)Continuing (i.e. permanent) position

    **What You Will Do**

    A new and exciting opportunity has arisen within the Financial Services Procurement Team for a Contract Administration Manager to join the service unit. In this role you will ensure equitable shared risk and benefits between vendors and the University by facilitating the review and value realisation of supply contracts.

    **Who You Are**

    As the successful candidate you will have the following knowledge, skills and experience:

    -   Proven experience in the development and implementation of policy and frameworks and applying required administrative processes
    -   Significant experience in negotiation and monitoring supply contractual arrangements and relationships
    -   Proven experience managing complex stakeholder relationships and an understanding of working within public sector environments
    -   Strong commercial and legal judgement

    You will have strong stakeholder engagement, communication and influencing skills as well as established problem solving skills. Additionally, you will have a proven ability to develop trust and understanding across all functions and levels.

    **Who you will work with**

    The role of Financial Services is to provide central procurement, accounting, business insight, advice and processing of financial transactions. The unit provides a high quality service while developing and maintaining excellent relationships with internal and external customers.

    Further information about the Service Unit and our team, please visit: <https://www.canterbury.ac.nz/about/finance/>

    **Why UC**

    The University of Canterbury is committed to promoting a world-class learning environment through research and teaching excellence, and has a vision statement of \"People Prepared to Make a Difference\". You will have the opportunity to work alongside members of a diverse academic community and enrich your own professional and personal development.

    For information about the range of fantastic benefits that UC offers including flexible work practices, study opportunities and generous superannuation and leave provisions, please visit us: <http://www.canterbury.ac.nz/joinus>
