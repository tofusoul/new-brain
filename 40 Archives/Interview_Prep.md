- Stressful Situation

Describe a time when you were faced with a stressful Situation that demonstrated your coping skills.

**Situation**

With no prior experience I was assigned a key role in small project team delivering a project for the ministry of education

**Task**

I was responsible for managing project information and changes on the a project that grew from an initial estimated value of \$1.2M with no defined scope to a 13-site project worth \$4.22M in contracted value and we were to deliver that within 3 months with the bulk of the works period falling on the xmas holidays with the aim of having the children occupy the classrooms by january 29th.

**Activity**

I was in constant communication with all members of the project, gathered what I understood to be their key needs and updated those information as rapidly as possible. I made sure with the help of the programme manager that I was included in all project communications so I can organise everything that came through for easy retrieval. I arranged to set up site IT and situated myself on site so I can develop an understanding of the salience of the various pieces of information. I spend all available spare time on preparing for what is required next so I can identify the roadblocks as soon as possible, and work through them. When things got overwhelming, I step back from the work for a few seconds, take some deep breaths, and head back into the work.

**Results**

We achieved code compliance and had the students occupy the classrooms on time. The ministry called it one of their \'miracle projects\', Corbel is now established as a prefered sub-contractor for the Ministry of Education.
