# Kitchen Improvement Projects

**Status:** Idea/Planning Phase
**Type:** Home Improvement

## Overview
Small kitchen improvement projects to enhance cooking experience and organization.

## Project Ideas

### Chopping Board for Sink
- [ ] #task Make a chopping board that fits into the sink
- **Benefit:** Easier food prep and cleanup
- **Design:** Custom fit to sink dimensions

### Alfalfa Sprouts
- [ ] #task Make alfalfa sprouts
- **Benefit:** Fresh, healthy sprouts for salads and sandwiches
- **Frequency:** Ongoing/regular batch production

### Vegetable Dumplings
- [ ] #task Make and freeze a large batch of vegetable dumplings
- **Benefit:** Quick, convenient meals ready to cook
- **Storage:** Freezer for long-term use

## Planning

### Materials Needed
- Chopping board materials (wood or plastic)
- Alfalfa seeds and sprouting containers
- Dumpling wrappers
- Vegetables for filling (cabbage, carrots, etc.)
- Freezer containers

### Equipment
- Sprouting jars or trays
- Dumpling press (optional)
- Freezer space organization
- Kitchen tools for preparation
