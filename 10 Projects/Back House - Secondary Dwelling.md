### Christchurch City Council Requirements

**Reference:** District Plan - Chapter 14.4 Minor Residential Buildings
**Link:** https://districtplan.ccc.govt.nz/pages/plan/Book.aspx?exhibit=districtplan&hid=330642

**Definition:** Minor residential unit where minor unit is a detached building on existing site containing only one residential unit.

#### Site Requirements
- **Minimum net site area:** 450m² (existing site containing both units)
- **Maximum site coverage:** 35% (includes all buildings - carports, garage)

#### Unit Size Requirements
- **Minimum gross floor area:** 35m²
- **Maximum gross floor area:** 80m²

#### Parking Requirements
- Parking areas of both units must be accessed from same access point

#### Outdoor Living Space
- **Total outdoor living space:** Minimum 90m² across entire site
- **Minimum dimension:** 5 meters
- **Can be provided as:**
  - Single continuous area, OR
  - Two separate spaces, provided each unit has outdoor living space:
    - Directly accessible from that unit
    - Minimum 30m² in area

**Note:** This requirement replaces general outdoor living space requirements in Rule 14.4.2.5
