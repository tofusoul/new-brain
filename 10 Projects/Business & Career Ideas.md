### Food Business

**Moon Shot:** Create a business that takes real steps toward giving general public a vegetarian lifestyle they're happy to stick with. Make vegetarian food as sexy, convenient, delicious, and nutritious as what's currently norm.

**Questions:**
- What's target opening date?
- What are costs?
  - Caravan or rent space?
  - Market stalls?
  - Container shop costs?
- What equipment available?
- Which culinary institutes are good?

**Possibilities:**
- Sweet Potato Balls
- Vegetarian Mince Rice/Noodles
- Chinese Dishes
- Vegetarian Curry

**Three kinds of people needed:**
1. Those who make things
2. Those who sell things
3. Those who look after money

**Connections:**
- Most working connections in construction
- Second-hand connections with food in Malaysia and Taiwan

### Lifestyle Business
**Characteristics:**
- Focus on passive income
- Maximize job satisfaction
- Low risk, moderate return
- Low capital, no investors
- Small market with low competition
- Linear or capped growth
- Can be done on your own

**Requirements:**
- Technical skills most important
- Enjoy process
- Broad skill set
- Interest in marketing
- Aim to work for both small and large companies

**Monetization Strategies:**
- Freemium
- Subscription
- Paid products
- Personal brand (consultancy gigs)
- Marketplace
- Advertising
- Donation

## Business Planning Framework

### Strategic Road Map
Document establishing plan for accomplishing strategic goal and describing why goal is in place.

**Difficulty:** Easy

**Steps:**
1. **Establish vision:** Write paragraph describing results of achieving plan
2. **Define goal:** State particular strategic project
3. **Analyze gap:** Describe current state vs desired state
4. **Develop tactical plan:** Detail resources and timeline to bridge gap

### For Each Business:
- Come up with a brief
- Infrastructure to build
- System for keeping track of accounts (beancount and fava)
- Setup system for recording and sharing key learning effortlessly
- Setup Nextcloud

### Business Plans
- Do this if it will incur costs
- What are competitions like?
- Immutable advantage
- How is market changing?
- Be first to market with minimum feature set

### Marketing Plan
Plan marketing before product:
- Research customers and their industry
- Find influential people
- Establish business relationships
- What businesses and people can I help?
- Contacts
- Loss leader/freemium
- Grandfathering (first users free)
- Free products to influential people
- Careful what you give away (taking back causes discontent)
- Think about advertising avenues

### Ideation Process
- Keep watch on constant pain points
- Solve a genuine problem
- Unfilled niches
- Hint to people that you want to start a business
