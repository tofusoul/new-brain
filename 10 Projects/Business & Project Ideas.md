## Project Ideas

### Food Business
**Concept:** Create a business that makes vegetarian lifestyle accessible and appealing to the general public.

**Moon Shot Goal:** Make vegetarian food as sexy, convenient, delicious, and nutritious as the current norm.

**Key Questions:**
- [ ] What's the target opening date?
- [ ] What are the costs? (Caravan or rent space? Market stalls?)
- [ ] How much do container shops cost?
- [ ] What equipment is available?
- [ ] Which culinary institutes are good to explore?

**Opening Menu Candidates:**
- Sweet Potato balls
- Vegetarian Mince Rice/Noodles
- Miengin House dishes
- Vegetarian Curry

**Status:** Concept phase - needs market research

## Business Planning Framework

### Value Proposition
- What is the problem?
- What itch does it scratch?

### Development Process
- Prototype: Tangible representation of idea
- Minimal Desirable Product: Someone should desire it
- Be prepared to accept feedback
- What people do is more reliable than what they say

### Considerations
- Infrastructure to build up
- System for recording and sharing key learning effortlessly
- Business plans (do this if incurring costs)
- Competition analysis
- Immutable advantage
- Market changes

### Monetization Options
- Support model (Richard Stallman)
- Freemium
- Subscription
- Paid products
- Personal brand (consultancy gigs)
- Marketplace
- Advertising
- Donation

### Business Type Preferences
- Lifestyle business
- Social enterprise
- Focus on passive income
- Maximize job satisfaction
- Low risk, moderate return
- Low capital, no investors
- Small market with low competition
- Linear or capped growth
- Can be done on your own
- Technical skills most important
- Enjoy the process
- Needs broad skill set
- Interest in marketing

- [ ] Solve a clear problem

- [ ] Have achievable scope
- [ ] Have realistic timeline
- [ ] Have necessary resources (skills, time, capital)
