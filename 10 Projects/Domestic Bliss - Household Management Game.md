# Domestic Bliss - Household Management Game

**Status:** Concept Phase
**Tagline:** A Little Game for Making Lives Together Better
**Type:** App/Game for Shared Living Spaces

## Core Concept

When a group of people share a living space, you have an opportunity to make your lives together better. This game is a tool that helps make it easier to make each other's lives better.

**Alternative to:**
- Living separate lives under one roof
- Silently causing grief through unspoken expectations
- Constant nagging and resentment

## Game Elements

### Chores
Regular tasks that maintain a good environment for everyone.

**Categories:**
- Daily
- Weekly
- Monthly
- Quarterly
- Yearly

### Bounties
Reward system for completing special tasks or going above and beyond.

### Habits
Track and reinforce good habits that benefit the household.

### Projects
Larger undertakings that require planning and coordination.

### Rewards
Earn rewards for consistent contribution and effort.

### Tie In With Big Picture
Connect daily tasks to long-term household goals and values.
