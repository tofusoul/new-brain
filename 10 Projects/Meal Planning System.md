# Meal Planning System

**Status:** Active
**Goal:** Create a system that makes regular meals more effortless and less time-consuming

## Overview
Develop a meal planning workflow that reduces the cognitive load and time spent deciding what to cook and eat.

## Approach

### Weekly Meal Planning
- Plan meals for the entire week
- Include batch cooking days
- Balance variety with practicality
- Consider seasonal ingredients and specials

### Batch Cooking Strategy
**Core items to batch cook:**
- Peanut butter
- Ragu (pasta sauce)
- Falafel
- Flat bread

### Shopping Organization
Organize shopping by store type:

**Use Up First:**
- Items in fridge/pantry that need consuming
- Check: Celery, Hulled Peanut, Carrots, Pumpkin, Apples, Potatoes

**Pico (Specialty Store):**
- Savory Yeast
- TVP mince

**Chinese Supermarket:**
- Frozen Garlic
- Onions
- Tofu
- Miso

**Supermarket:**
- Canned Tomato
- Frozen Spinach
- Croissants

**Veggie Shops:**
- Whatever looks good and is on special

## Week Structure
- **Sunday:** Cooking and prep day
- **Weekdays:** Use leftovers and quick meals
- **Weekend:** More elaborate meals, salad nights
