# Mushroom Farm

**Start Date:** 2020-09-13
**Status:** Experimental (paused)
**Type:** Small-scale Oyster Mushroom Production

## Overview
First attempt at growing mushrooms from a grow kit birthday present. Experimented with Oyster mushrooms using grain spawn and oak wood substrate.

## History & Experiments

### Batch 0 (2020-09-13)
- **Strain:** Oyster Mushrooms
- **Grain Spawn:** Sporeshift
- **Substrate:** Disintegrated Oak Wood Pallet (should have been pine - oak was harder to source and less suitable)
- **Mixing Method:** Bucket and Stick (not ideal - stick needed to be pointy)

### Results & Yields
| Flush | Date | Yield | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|-------|
| 1st Flush | 2020-11-13 | 500g | First bag |
| 1st Flush | 2020-11-13 | 220g | Second bag + some 2nd flush from first |
| 2nd Flush | 2020-11-23 | 476g | Second bag |
| 3rd Flush | 2020-12-19 | 319g | First bag |

### Issues Encountered
- **Overheating:** Some fruiting bodies died due to excessive heat
- **Contamination:** One grain spawn bag didn't propagate (bag was scrunched, smelled bad)
- **Temperature:** Incubation too cold initially, then too hot in summer fruiting
- **Spore buildup:** Significant spore release during 2nd flush
- **Equipment:** Improper mixing tools, poor sprayer for humidity control

## Production Planning (Future)

### Production Target
- **Goal:** 10kg/week
- **Space Required:** ~15m² total (Lab 5m², Incubation 3m², Fruiting 5m²+)

### Setup Options

**Option 1: Trial Setup**
- Small tent for incubation and fruiting
- Small humidifier with timer
- Requires daily airing
- Cost: ~$100+ (tent + humidifier)

**Option 2: Home Scale (10m²)**
- One large tent or two separate tents (hydroponic for incubation, PVC for fruiting)
- Production: 10-20kg/week

**Option 3: Rental Scale (30m²)**
- Professional setup with separate prep, colonization, and fruiting areas
- Rental cost: $500+/month

### Lab Requirements
- Mixing & inoculation area
- Spawn storage
- Easy-clean waterproof surfaces
- Positive pressure ventilation with HEPA filter
- Laminar flow hood
- Scale, mixer, barrels, refrigerator
- Moisture meter, CO2 meter

### Incubation Requirements
- Dark, insulated, warm
- Easy cleaning
- Temperature: 20-24°C
- Shelving, heating, power supply
- Minimum: 3m²

### Fruiting Requirements
- Shipping container, hydroponics tent, or custom build
- Lighting
- Airflow (CO2 < 900ppm)
- Humidifier (85%)
- Temperature: 12-20°C or 18-26°C (strain dependent)
- Shelving, power, water
- Minimum: 5m² for 10-20kg/week
