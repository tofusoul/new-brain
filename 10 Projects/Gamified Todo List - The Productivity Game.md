# Gamified Todo List - The Productivity Game

**Status:** Concept/Planning Phase
**Type:** App Development

## Overview
A gamified productivity system that connects tasks to goals, happiness, and fulfillment through gaming mechanics.

## Core Philosophy
- Work with people in your life to structure time meaningfully
- Reduce things to do to improve focus on fulfillment
- Measure tasks against broader goals and fulfillment
- Connect action to happiness and laziness

## Gameplay Mechanics

### Idea 1: Three Goals Challenge

**Workflow:**
1. User identifies 3 goals to accomplish (SMART principle)
2. Share with a loved and respected person
3. The reviewer:
   - Can veto one goal and challenge with another
   - User tweaks parameters until excited about it
   - Reviewer accepts modified goals
4. When a goal is accomplished, set another and repeat

**App Features:**
- Goal setting interface
- Peer review system (socializing how to achieve goals)
- Analytics and tracking
- Outlining/analysis/dependency modeling
- Paths rewarded for simplicity
- Time boxing/Pomodoro integration
- Gamification elements

### Todo List Game

**Core Principles:**
- Hold user to "do as they say, and say as they do"
- One task or project presented at a time (no distractions)
- Timer-based urgency for extra focus
- Virtual object rewards and visual track of efforts
- User tasks placed in organized bins

**Scoring System:**
- No points earned unless timeframe specified
- Points given for ordering tasks into sequence of actions
- Tasks in bins are spit out one at a time
- If action is doable: default 10-minute timer
- Estimated time can be specified
- Timer ticks down
  - Points start to diminish after 10 minutes
  - Sub-tasks add extra 5 minutes each
- Shields can be applied if tasks can't be accomplished in time (at a cost)
- Each tick gives materials to construct things and hire minions (in-game)

**Social Features:**
- Other users can give kudos for actions
- Finished tasks create sequences that can be modified and shared as step-by-step checklists
- Shareable with time estimates

**Additional Features:**
- Bullshit calling mechanism
- Gardening game variant?
- Appropriate metaphors for game aspects

## Story Concept (Optional)

**Setting:**
Earth's bio arrived at a level of disharmony making it uninhabitable. Humans retreated to a space vessel resembling a giant brain, living over and over again.

**Turning Point:** 21st century
Sphere drifts across space for 20,000 years, arriving at first inhabitable planet.

**Premise:**
Leaders decide to settle slowly and rightly. Need help. You've been awakened from dream to help build first settlement as a miner.

**Mechanics:**
- Day-to-day life and decisions earn dream credits
- Dream credits converted to real-life substances on planet
- Befriend local tribe and other awakened humans
- Mine, grow gardens, share and learn from locals
- Defend against threats by earning credits

**Story Hook:**
"Heads: wake up, we've been observing you... I see you still have dreams after these long 20,000 years. There's not many of you left. The system has claimed many, but we're finally near liberation. We need you. We are building a new home for us all. It will take time and people with dreams and the will to carry them through."

**Endgame:**
When life runs its course on sphere, claim home in land of the freed for one last lifetime as a real human again.
