- Mushroom~Soup~

Version 1: Tuesday 17 April 2012 **Used:**

- Shimeiji Mushroom

- Oyster Mushroom

- Shitake Mushroom

**Steps:**

- Saute mushroom in butter with cracked black pepper till it sweats.

- Blend with some water.

- Reheat and serve

**Notes:** \> It worked pretty well, but it was very filling - and consumming more than a bowl made me feel a bit unwell. The colour was a bit pale. I believe I should use only portobello mushroom next time. I should also keep track of yields. This yielded about half a pot.
