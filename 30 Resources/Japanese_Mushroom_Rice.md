Japanese Mushroom Rice **Thu Apr 19 23:37:23 SGT 2012** **Used:**

- Fresh Shitake Mushroom.

- Mixed grain rice.

- Wakame (baby kelp.

- Kikoman Soysauce

**Steps:**

- Saute mushroom in oil with white pepper and soysauce till soft.

- cooked with soaked rice and wakame.

**Notes:** \> It worked pretty well. But the fresh mushroom wasn\'t fragrant enough and should have used japanese short grain rice instead of mixed grain.
