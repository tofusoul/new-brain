- Lentil Burger 

based on [this](https://www.makingthymeforhealth.com/classic-lentil-burgers/) and [this](https://www.food.com/recipe/vegan-lentil-burgers-224363#activity-feed) recipe.

- Ingredients

-   Puy Lentils (cooked in a salt broth with star anise and bay)

-   onions (finely chopped in food processor)

-   Carrots (cooked until soft)

-   cajun spice

-   tomato paste

-   cooked carrots and potatos

-   pepper

-   oat flour

-   bread crumbs

-   balsamic vinigar

-   coconut oil

-   shredded coconut

-   kelp powder

- Note

-   over cooked the lentils , would be nicer if it wasn\'t (they cook pretty fast after a soaking)
