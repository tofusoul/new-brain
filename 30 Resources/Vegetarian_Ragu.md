**Steps:**

- Brown meat alternative in sauce pan.

- Deglaze the pan with wine, reduce by half

- cook in milk

- remove froth on surface

- cook for a long time (6 hours)

- make the tomato compot (cook for an hour)

- fry in olive oil till complete
