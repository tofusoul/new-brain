- The 4 Cs

- Context Diagrams

- What is the scope of the software system we are using?

- who is using it?

- what are they doing?

- what system integrations does it need to support

- Container Diagrams

- Sketch out the system

**\***

- Component Diagrames

- Code Diagrams

- Stop the desigin process when:

- you understand the significant architectural drivers

- requirements

- quality attributes

- constraints

- understand the context and scope of what you are building

- Understand the significant design descisions

- technology

- modularity etc

- Have a way to communicate your vision to other people

- confident that you are comfortable with the risks associated with building the software
