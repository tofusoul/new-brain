= Tofu in Fermented Tofu Sauce = Version 0 : **Used:**

- Slices of Tofu

- Fermented Tofu

- Ginger

- Sesame Oil

- Cooking Oil

**Steps:**

- Fry Tofu on both sides until golden.

- Saute ginger during the last stages browning tofu.

- Desolve Fermented tofu in water or wine.

- Cook until all of the liquid has evapporated.

- Finish with some sesame oil.
