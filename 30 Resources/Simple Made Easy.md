- Notes from [Rich Hickey\'s 2011 Talk](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SxdOUGdseq4)

- Simple

- from simplex

- one fold one braid vs [[Complex]]

- means focus

- not too interleaved

- Easy

- lies near

- Reachable

- in current skill set.

- Familiarity

- Near our capabilities

- Nothing new is near

- Easy is relative to the person

- Artifacts vs Constructs

- software is built from constructs but delivers artifacts

- the builder is focused on the elegance of the construct rather than

the simplicity of the artifacts

- Limits

- We can only hope to make realiable things we can understand

- we can only consider a few things at a time

- The things that are intertwined need to be considered together\[fn:1\]

- Complex

- Complexity undermines our understanding

- Change

- Your ability to understand how things are connected is critical to

creating change without fear

- You have to be without fear because your team depends on you for

confidence

- you can\'t pretend not to fear when you actually do, people can smell

it from miles away

- Speed

- Emphasizing ease gives early speed

- Ignoring comlexity will slow you down over the long haul

- Throwaway or trivial projects nothing much matters

- Fix problems or Improve Capabilities

- I don\'t know, I don\'t want to know...
