- Brief

Almost blind, deaf and mute given the nature of his birth. Cyberware replacing his vision, auditory sense and voice, one of his amputated arms and one of his amputated legs. Through years of contemplation of suffering, he has internalised emptiness which allows him to act without fearing pain. Escaping capture from having murdered thousands as leader of a cult with the aim of ending all suffering by ending sentience -- he has found his skills well suited for the life of a rigger.

- Story

His mum, while 9 months pregnant with him, was caught in the middle of a major conflict. On order to instill fear in the entire populous of his mother\'s nation, the invading soldiers found her at the height of their frenzy to artfully create hell for the residence of her town, the town that has fought most ferociously against them.

She was found by a platoon of war worn men who decided to cut him out of her womb and make her watch as she bleeds to death -- a foot ball match using him as the ball, while some of them raped her in orifices cut out of her flesh.

One of the soldiers, powerless to resist the madness by means other than obstaining from the torture, noticed the faint traces of life left in him, saved him and raised him. He became a great general and furnished the boy with an elites life with education, wealth status and connections.

Contemplating suffering, he came a full understanding of emptiness. he established a cult following among the youth of the elite. They contemplated life through the lense of suffering and settled on a path to end all suffering but ending sentience as we know it. Restoring the world to silence. When the world\'s authorities came crashing down on his cult. all his friends perished in the crackdown while he narrowly escaped with their help.

Being severed entirely from his past, and halted entirely from his trajectory. he is recontemplating life and suffering by necessity. Being a shadowrunner has given him a path to rebuild life and hone the masteries he has gathered with his past. He continues to contemplate suffering and toil, and there is no better place to witness this.
