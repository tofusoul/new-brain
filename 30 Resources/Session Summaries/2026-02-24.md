## OpenClaw Context Management

### 📊 What We Learned About Context

**Per-session context:**
- Each OpenClaw session has its own conversation history
- Context doesn't flood automatically — sessions are isolated
- Compaction happens when limits are reached
- New session = fresh start, no old context

**Daily note vs. conversation history:**
- Daily notes are YOUR territory in Obsidian — you manage them
- Conversation history stays in OpenClaw session — ephemeral
- Bean can READ your files when asked, but doesn't auto-modify daily notes
- Bean can SEARCH your files for context, but doesn't auto-triage Inbox

**Best practice:**
- Continue same session for ongoing work (preserves full history)
- Use `/export-session` before starting major new topic
- New session when switching completely to different work
