- 10 years ago Today. I fell utterly in love. It wasn\'t just a crush, it wasn\'t just butterflies from admiring someone beautiful and it wasn\'t just the excitement of connecting with someone deeply as humans in unhindered moments . This was something I\'d never thought I\'d experience -- that fabled love at first sight!

I was floored by the way you smiled so radiantly, how deeply and carefully you thought about the world and life, and still be as light as a feather about being alive. I was floored by how you spent time doing kind things humbly, little and big, to help those in need and finding out quietly and calmly where you could contribute. I loved the strange blend of shyness, confidence and openness you express. I love how you can do so much so relaxedly, and how you are amused at how ridiculous I am, and how you laugh so heartily about it.

We have a beautiful little boy together. Dill, you and me have had so much fun already in this cozy little corner of the world. We see the seasons change in our garden and have uncountable beautiful moments together. You take care of way more than you should, and when I\'m lost, you always have a way to ground me and make me see things for what they are. There is so much about you that you only show me. I am so honored to have the privilege of being that person to you.

We have lost a beautiful baby together. I could not believe your courage when you had to give birth to Elliot after finding out that his heart stopped beating the way we did. I love your incredible strength, and how we are in sync in how we would mourn for him, and cherish this fleeting time we have with him.

I will probably always be a stubborn, weird idiot, but you have made me a better idiot each day. Better at coping and navigating the strange seas called life. I don\'t know what I\'ll do without you!

We\'ve crossed oceans together and apart. I know now why love is talked about in such mysterious ways. Being in love with you is so easy, simple and sweet it\'s like breathing air, or eating noodles-- so effortless it\'s \'meant to be\' (like having feet). You know me more than I do, and most times I can\'t think about life without you right there next to me as if we are not two, but one. You asked me when we first got together, \'how can you be so sure about me being the perfect girl?\' It\'s a mystery! But I\'ve never been so right about anything!

I love you, and thank you for our 10 years so far!
