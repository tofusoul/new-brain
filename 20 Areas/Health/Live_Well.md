Writing this becasue I have been intensely unhappy despite the fact that life has been objectively idylic. I am married to Lil, the girl of my dreams, she\'s everything I need and want and more; We have a child Dylan, who is sweet and smart, loves us and growingup nicely; We have an almost quater acre 3 bedroom house with all the space you could want as a family of 3. We have a comfortable income, living in a country with some safty nets. I have a \'career\' of sorts. What is it about my life that makes me intensely unhappy?

I have been here once, when I was much younger. I had thought then that it was about my loneliness and isolation. This time I have family with me, a soul mate and a beatuful child.

Could be because we lost Elliot, but my feeling is that that\'s not quite it. Feels like what happend with Elliot is a wake up call. I can\'t keep doing what ever it is for much longer. But what is it that I need to head away from? Could it be striaghtforward?

What I felt was in common between my depressed days and my current days is this feeling of helplessness, like there is nothing I can do to better the things I need bettering. But at the same time, I lack confidence in what I believe ought to change in my life. I could not view with any certainty, the kind of things I was identifying as the reasons. I feel stuck, but feel powerless to escape. I feel what I was having to do (school then, work now) was evidently pointless, but I can\'t do anything else (they were not options).

Maybe that is it. That feeling of not being in my elements. The learnt helplessness

I wonder if there are ways out of this feeling?
