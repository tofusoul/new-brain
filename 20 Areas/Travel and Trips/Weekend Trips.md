# Any Time

## Woolshed Creek Hut Track (Mt Somers area)
- Access from Woolshed Creek car park (easier for families).
- To hut: 1.5 - 2.5 hr (3.5 km) one way. Good for an overnight stay or a there-and-back day trip.
- Track features historic pack track, a mine entrance, and a "water race tunnel".
- Longer option: Woolshed Creek and Miners Track Loop (5-6 hr, 11.4 km).
- [ ] Book Woolshed Creek Hut via DOC website.
- [ ] Check DOC website for track conditions & alerts before going.

## Mt Sunday with Methvan Hot Pools (Ōpuke Thermal Pools & Spa)
- **Mt Sunday:**
  - Approx 1.5 - 2 hr drive from CHCH to Mt Potts Road (turn-off for Mt Sunday).
  - Walk: ~1-1.5 hr return, relatively easy. Famous as Edoras from Lord of the Rings. Stunning panoramic views.
  - Good for families, but exposed, so check weather and be prepared.
- **Ōpuke Thermal Pools & Spa (Methven):**
  - Approx 30-45 min drive from Mt Sunday area to Methven.
  - Family-friendly "Discovery Pools" with fun features (crazy river, plunge pool).
  - Also has Tranquility pools (adults - check age restrictions) and private Solar Tubs.

## Lake Daniell Track (Manson Nicholls Memorial Hut)
- Location: Lewis Pass area, ~2-2.5 hr drive from CHCH to the track start near Boyle River.
- Track: 8.4 km one way (2.5-3 hrs each way) to Manson Nicholls Hut by Lake Daniell.
- Mostly flat/gentle gradient through beautiful beech forest. Excellent first overnight tramp for families.
- **To-Do:**
  - [ ] Book Manson Nicholls Hut via DOC website (Hut tickets/pass usually required).
  - [ ] Be prepared for cold weather (Lewis Pass area can be cold year-round) and sandflies (especially near the lake).

## Omarama Clay Cliffs with Hot Tubs Omarama
- **Omarama Clay Cliffs:**
  - Approx 10km west of Omarama township. Short, easy walk into unique, towering geological formations.
  - Suitable for families; wear good shoes. Small entry fee often payable at an honesty box (private land).
- **Hot Tubs Omarama:**
  - Private, wood-fired hot tubs using fresh mountain water. Saunas available.
  - Located on Main Omarama Twizel Highway (State Hwy 8).
- **Notes:**
  - Drive from CHCH: ~3.5-4 hours.
  - Omarama is part of the Aoraki Mackenzie International Dark Sky Reserve – great for stargazing if staying overnight.
- **To-Do:**
  - [ ] Book accommodation in Omarama if staying overnight.
  - [ ] Book Hot Tubs Omarama (bookings essential).

## Lake Brunner and Carew Falls
- Location: West Coast, ~2.5-3 hr drive from CHCH via Arthur's Pass (SH73). Main town: Moana.
- **Lake Brunner:**
  - Largest lake in the North Westland. Good for fishing, boating, kayaking.
  - Several family-friendly short walks around Moana village. Glow worm grottos can be found nearby.
- **Carew Falls Track:**
  - Near Arnold Dam, a short drive from Moana.
  - Easy ~20-30 min return walk (as per original note) through native forest to a waterfall. Very suitable for families.
- **To-Do:**
  - [ ] Consider accommodation in Moana or nearby if making it a weekend trip.

# New Suggestions (Christchurch-based, Family-Friendly, Low-Cost/Free Focus)

## Akaroa & Banks Peninsula
- ~1.5 hr drive from CHCH. Scenic historic French town.
- **Low-Cost/Free Activities:**
  - **Akaroa Waterfront & Childrens Bay:** Stroll, play, paddle (if you have own gear). Free.
  - **Short Walks:** Many options around the town and bays with nice views. Free.
  - **Akaroa Museum:** Learn about local history (check for entry fee, often donation-based).
  - **The Giant's House:** (Entry fee applies) A unique mosaic and sculpture garden, visually engaging for kids.
  - **Pohatu Penguins (Flea Bay):** While tours have a cost, researching their conservation efforts can be educational. Driving to Flea Bay (check road conditions) is scenic in itself.
  - **Dolphin Spotting from Shore:** Less predictable than a tour, but always a possibility. Free.
- **Higher Cost Options (if budget allows):**
  - Hector's Dolphin Watching Cruise (e.g., Black Cat Cruises, ~2 hrs).
  - Pohatu Penguins guided tours.
- **Notes:**
  - Variety of accommodation (book ahead). Many cafes and eateries.
- [ ] Check Akaroa Museum entry.
- [ ] Research Pohatu Penguins tour costs/details if interested.
- [ ] Look into local walks on Akaroa.com or DOC website.
- [ ] Book accommodation if staying.

## Hanmer Springs
- ~1.5-2 hr drive north of CHCH. Alpine spa village.
- **Low-Cost/Free Activities:**
  - **Hanmer Forest Park:** Numerous walking and mountain biking trails. The "Forest Amble" is a sculpture walk great for kids. Free.
  - **Village Playgrounds:** Good public playgrounds. Free.
  - **River walks/exploring:** Explore the banks of the Hanmer River. Free.
- **Paid Activities:**
  - **Hanmer Springs Thermal Pools & Spa:** Main attraction with family activity area, hydroslides. (Entry fee applies).
  - **Hanmer Springs Animal Park:** (Entry fee applies).
  - **Mini Golf:** (Cost involved).
- **Notes:**
  - Wide range of accommodation (holiday parks good for budget) - book ahead.
  - Good selection of eateries.
- [ ] Check DOC website for Hanmer Forest Park walks.
- [ ] Check costs for Thermal Pools/Animal Park if interested.
- [ ] Book accommodation if staying.

## Kaikōura
- ~2.5 hr drive north of CHCH. Coastal town, mountain views, marine life.
- **Low-Cost/Free Activities:**
  - **Kaikōura Peninsula Walkway:**
    - Point Kean Seal Colony: See seals up close (keep distance!). Free.
    - Clifftop Walk: Stunning coastal & mountain views, chance to spot marine life. Various lengths. Free.
  - **Ohau Point Seal Colony Lookout:** View seals on rocks from SH1 platform. (Waterfall pool currently closed - check DOC). Free.
  - **Beachcombing:** Explore stony beaches. Free.
  - **Fyffe House (exterior & grounds):** Historic whaling station. Free to see from outside.
  - **Mount Fyffe Tracks:** Various hiking options, from shorter walks to a challenging hike to Mt Fyffe Hut (great views). Free.
- **Stand-Up Paddleboarding (SUP):**
  - Possible in sheltered bays. Gear rental would be a cost.
- **Notes:**
  - Famous for whale watching/dolphin tours (these are higher cost).
  - Book accommodation (holiday parks are a good budget option) well in advance.
- [ ] Check DOC website for current status of Ohau Waterfall track & Mt Fyffe tracks.
- [ ] Research SUP rental costs if interested.

## Castle Hill (Kura Tāwhiti) & Arthur's Pass
- **Castle Hill (Kura Tāwhiti Conservation Area):**
  - ~1.5 hr drive from CHCH (on SH73).
  - Explore unique limestone rock formations. Great for scrambling and imaginative play.
  - Easy walking tracks. Free entry.
  - Exposed area; prepare for weather. Good footwear.
- **Arthur's Pass National Park (further ~30-45 mins):**
  - **Devil's Punchbowl Falls Walk:** ~1 hr return to impressive 131m waterfall (many stairs). Free.
  - **Arthur's Pass Walking Track (to Dobson Memorial):** ~1-1.5 hr return, forest and alpine views. Free.
  - **Bridal Veil Falls Walk:** ~30 min return, easier option. Free.
  - **DOC Visitor Centre:** Displays and info. Free.
  - *Caution:* Alpine weather, can change quickly. Do not feed Kea.
- **Notes:**
  - Can be a long day trip. For a weekend, limited accommodation in Arthur's Pass village (book well ahead) or towns en route.
  - Excellent for nature exploration and hiking.
- [ ] Check track conditions on DOC website before going.
