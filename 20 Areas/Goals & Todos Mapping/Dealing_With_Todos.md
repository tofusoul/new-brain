- Method

-   Put everything that I feel I should pay attention to in one of 3 places:

    -   The longlist / Short list journal
    -   The giant inbox on the computer
    -   Google keep

-   Set aside some time to go through the items one by one and sincerely decide what to do with each one.

-   If it can be done within 5 min, do it now, doesn\'t matter if it\'s the most important thing or not

- Principles

Most productivity systems follow similar principles, you need to be able to:

-   focus is the only way to get more time.

    -   The goal is to focus and reduce the amount of switch tasking and distracted stretches of work.

-   Put aside thoughts, ideas and competing priorities as they pop up on your head

-   a way of systematically going through the items in order to be assured that noting things down doesn\'t mean ignoring it.

-   use of calendar to box away time

-   simple and easy to use

-   adapts to your way of working

-   everything in one place

-   eliminate distracting environments.

-   alt-c to check lists
