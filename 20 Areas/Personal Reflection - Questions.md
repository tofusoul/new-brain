# Personal Reflection - Questions

**Author:** Andrew Shih
**Type:** Self-Inquiry Prompts

## Overview
Questions for deep personal reflection on authenticity, possessions, and living life on one's own terms.

## Reflection Questions

### Electronic Devices & Children's Development
Research topic on the impact of electronic devices on children's development.

### Authenticity & Expectations
- **Question:** What are the ideas that you hold onto because you have been told you need to?
- **Question:** Are there any possessions you have or things that you have done because of what others (society, peers, family etc.) expected of you?

### Life Design
- **Question:** How is external expectation impacting your life?
- **Question:** What could you stop, start or change today to live life more on your terms?

## Purpose
These questions are tools for:
- Examining authenticity of beliefs and actions
- Identifying external pressures and expectations
- Making conscious choices about life direction
- Letting go of inauthentic possessions or commitments

## Usage
Use these questions periodically (weekly, monthly, quarterly) for:
- Life audits and reviews
- Decision-making clarity
- Authenticity checks
- Identifying areas for change
