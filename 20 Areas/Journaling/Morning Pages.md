# Morning Pages
> *Record down the day as it goes, stop rumination noise from overwhelming*
- Write stream of consciousness thoughts for as 15 min each session

- The goal is to clear the head of the distracting thoughts

- don\'t worry about what you write here it\'s just to chanel the stream out.

- keeping it is important to the mind-- for your mind to know that that thought is being placed processed.

- How to Morning Pages [from the creater, Julia Cameron](https://juliacameronlive.com/basic-tools/morning-pages/)

-   3 pages of long for stream of consciousness writing
-   Clear the mind, vacuumed for the dusty corner of the mind
-   write down 'cloud thoughts' just what is consciousness
