# Productivity

**Status:** Ongoing Responsibility
**Scope:** Personal productivity systems and techniques

## Time Management Techniques

### Pomodoro Technique
A method of timeboxing to better manage time.

**Characteristics:**
- Promotes focus on tasks
- Makes time feel longer (subjective)
- 25-minute focused work sessions
- 5-minute breaks between sessions
- Longer break after 4 pomodoros

**Benefits:**
- Improved focus
- Better time awareness
- Regular breaks prevent burnout
- Clear task completion markers
