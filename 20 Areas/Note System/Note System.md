- System for Todos and Notes

- For action x, ask the following questions

-   why am I doing x

-   if I am doing x because of y, why do I need to do y?

-   if I am doing y because of z, then why z?

-   if I feel z is important to me, do x

-   else, get rid of it from your life.

-   If I\'m going to do x: ask the following

    -   What is the next thing I am supposed to do with this
    -   When am I going to do that
    -   Where do I keep it till then?
