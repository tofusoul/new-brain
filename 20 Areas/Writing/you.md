---
title: You
date: 2024-01-30
tags: poem
---

Cicadas creek, rain of pettles weep;
dark night's moon waxes and wanes without seing the world that sees it, 
I felt a heartbeat without having heard it
I am the world and all there is in it, but is the world me with nobody in it. 
is that a sunset or my heart sinking
is that a bent spoon or a spoon with no meaning?
the same star glowed on our father's fathers
the same love flowed from mothers to daughters
I can't see it without your hands on my shoulders
I wake and sleep, 
to tears on window panes
and the suicide blue of the deepest skies
if not for you
who am I?
