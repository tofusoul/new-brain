I can't, for the life of me, see my own face. I have tried many mirrors, selfie cams, flipping through old photographs, facebook photos from family and friends. Where I am supposed to see me, smiling, laughing crying, struggling, and all these faces that we make through the adventures in our lives-- I see a man/ boy/ infant etc. With no face. 

You can't imagine the creepy welling up of fear when this happens to you. What is this supposed to mean? My face being erased from my world. Erased for no-one else but me. 

I wasn't born without a face. I've been told my face is basically as it was. But I have no idea what that is like now. I can't feel my smile, can't feel my cries. Even when I scream in this utter fear, I can't picture my own screaming face.
