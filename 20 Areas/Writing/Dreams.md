#Dreamer

When you get called a dreamer, people are generally not-so-subtly alluding to the impracticality of your aspirations. Maybe only in a dream will you get to live those aspirations.

I get called a dreamer lots growing up. I\'m 4o soon. I still dream, I just talk about it less and less.

More and more, I feel what people meant when they had plead for me to be \'more pragmatic\'. It means \'you have no idea how much it will take to make that happen.\' Forget about it, move on, live your days. You have children, mortgages and bills to pay, if you want your wage growth to exceed inflation, and be worry free when you retire, look after number one, you and yours. To be sure, and safe and secure in that sphere, it\'s struggle enough.
