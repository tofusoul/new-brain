I have now been on this earth for more than 44 years. Where once history is just what I read about, I have now lived a decent chunck of the kinds of change that is effectively what history consists of. I am now a speck in the chunk of human history that have felt enough of change to have a real substantive feelings of our place in time. Enough to feel like I can talk about it all and not just missed the entire point of what has been (if I may). 

I am feeling a lot about these currents these days, as we are on the precipice of massive global change that a lot of us who have lived though the same time period will no doubt be feeling. My hope in writing is to exorcese some thought demons by putting them on paper and start conversations with you (so the demon becomes just thoughts), with it we can maybe better build the parts of our own stories to come, without the FUD that comes with Chaotic currents. And hope to navigate with some of you to a good place in parts yet to be charted. Some of the principles that those before us have built this world on, have become real monsters, just as Nationalism and Industrialisation was before World War II. We need to think about these things before it's too late.  

Ideas matter. Ideas we realize matter even more. I don't want to realize the wrong ideas for where I am in the greater scheme of things- Even if it's just in my little corner of the world. Small problems can often be resolved through just perserverence and effort. Big problems need not only that, but also folks aligning with each other in their ideas, and in their efforts to realise those ideas. In writing and sharing, I hope to shape those ideas into ones that that we can put persistance and effort into together, to solve the problems that we can't solve on our own. 

Good ideas arn't good ideas forever, and I have been living out a lot of ideas that were good, and maybe still good, but so often they don't feel that way, and I've not made the space to examine them. Here I hope to look at them with you. 

For most of these articles I am going to write about different aspects of what happend in the world since I was born, relate my own experiences about those things, and say a little about why they are important for the way things are in the world now, and what they might mean for the future.

# Rise of China
If the west has an all out war with China, I do not think the west can win. There is only one thing that we need to consider--  supply Chains. We'd like to think intelligence, strategy and valor, training, attitude, culture, and values and fighting for the right things, are what won the war for the Allies. It kinda looks like that on the western front against the Nazis, we invented Radars, put together the dunkirk evacuation, radars, bletchly park, the manhatan project. There are real people, with real valor, with amazing innovations and strategies that could have only come together for a fighting force with the Anglo American Culture of the time. But if the Axis had won, they would be talking about the superiority of their own cultures, as the strategies and weapons they had could only have been the results of the greatness of their people. Genesides aside, some of those values could easily be concieved of as being admirable in the same way and they certainly felt their early victories were a result of those things.  

There is no doubt the Allies had more tanks, more bombers, more fighters, more pilots, more artilary, more fuel, more homefront support. More of everything. More able to destroy the opponent. The Nazi eastern front war against the Soviets is probably a more clear illustration. Soviet was able to create an endless stream of their tank forces to push the Nazi's all the way back to Berlin. 

Not discrediting the real heroes who fight in the war. But if the Allies did not have security and stability in their supply lines, especially that of fuel and ammunition, whatever strategic, cutlural, intelligence advantage they had, will only last as long as the supplies will allow.

The simple fact is, China these days, can make more of everything. Even if their weaponry is mildy inferior to the west, they have and will have more of it. The worst case for them is a WWII soviet style victory with heavy losses.
