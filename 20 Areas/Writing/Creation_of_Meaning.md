- Creation of Meaning

#writing

Before there were living things, sun sets and sun rises have no meaning. It means the end of the night it means the end of daylight, but it also means other things. It means dinner time, it means goodbye, it means all the things it means.

It could be said that the meaning of a sunset is given, we understand it via some form of receiving -- Some one pointing at the twilight sun, some encyclopaedia article about planetary motion, some piece of writing about dawn and dusk. Even something specific to you, recieved and circumstantial,
