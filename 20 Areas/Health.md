## Nutrition & Diet

### Dietary Approaches

#### Intermittent Fasting
- **Approach**: 16/8 fasting (16 hours fasting, 8-hour eating window) to start
- **Experience**: Fasting seems to work well
- **Ketosis Benefits**:
  - Increased clarity and alertness
  - Better energy levels during physical activity
  - Weight management support
- **Historical Results**:
  - 20-hour fast resulted in 4kg loss
  - Achieved lowest weight: 86kg (from starting 92.4kg)
  - Consistent morning weight: ~86-87kg

#### Slow Carb Diet
- **Source**: Tim Ferriss's "The 4-Hour Body"
- **Status**: Previously worked well but needs better implementation
- **Notes**: Requires more disciplined adherence
