# Existential Crisis: A Recurring Struggle with Meaning and Self

This note summarizes the core elements of my ongoing existential crisis, based on patterns across my journal entries, mental health reflections, and daily logs. It's not a one-time event but a persistent "spiritual hollow" or "severe emptiness" that flares up during transitions, low-energy periods, or milestones (e.g., turning 40 in [[2020]]). At its heart, it's a tension between inner drives for authenticity, novelty, and impact, and outer realities like family duties, societal expectations, and unstructured time—leaving me feeling "stuck," "frozen," or "not functioning" in a seemingly meaningless world.

## Key Feelings and Manifestations
- **Emotional Core**: Profound frustration, confusion, hopelessness, and self-doubt. Often described as "so bored it hurts," "empty shell," or nihilistic ("particles smashing through space," "ultimate meaninglessness"). Includes guilt over these emotions, self-hatred ("I hate myself, and I can’t say it"), and overwhelm that paralyzes action.
- **Indecision and Overwhelm**: Constant "Buridan's Ass" (inability to choose between options), monkey mind jumping between thoughts, and time-blindness (e.g., ADHD-like traits). Feels like being "wired but wrongly wired," isolated, and inadequate in roles (dad, husband, son).
- **Triggers**: Aging/finitude (halfway through life, unpredictable death, aging parents), neuro-divergence (distractibility, existential glitches), and societal alienation (capitalism's exploitation, inauthentic "masks," global tensions like China-Taiwan).

Links: See [[mind_problems]] and [[Problems]] for detailed lists of feelings; [[Not_Functioning]] for neuro-divergence notes; [[00 Periodic/Journal]] for raw daily expressions.

## Root Causes I Identify
- **Lack of Structure and Clarity**: No deadlines, objectives, or frameworks to prioritize tasks—everything feels like "competing ideas" without a path. Motivated but uninspired; know what I *don't* want (plodding organizations, isolation) but unclear on what I *do* (beyond "meaningful, novel ways to give back").
- **Paradox of Privilege**: "Happy, healthy family" and "enough to get by," yet a "misfit" breaking under the "weight of nothing." Echoes of past traumas (e.g., family deaths) amplify this.
- **Broader Existential Questions**: "What does it mean to exist? Do numbers/nations exist?" Doubting instincts leads to freezing; shoulds (societal, self-imposed) feel like "the death of me."

Links: [[2020]] for milestone panic; [[Morning Pages]] for philosophical musings; [[Captured Notes]] for recurring themes like "no one is judging you" as self-compassion reminders.

## Patterns and Search for Resolution
- **Interconnections**: Ties into family commitments (e.g., harmony with Lil, Dylan, Luca), tech/societal critiques (e.g., AI's emotional bonds, work's future), and rituals for peace. Often ends with resolve: determination over half-done things, letting "meaninglessness roll on."
- **What I Need**: Deadlines/objectives, inspiring frameworks tying life threads, real connections, mastery (skill-building), and daily rituals (meditation, writing, nature, family time). Glimmers of hope in authenticity ("live my authentic life," "do what I was born to do").
- **Philosophical Lens**: Draws from Daoism, Stoicism, pragmatism—questioning meaning while affirming action ("doing it is figuring it out"). A call for integration: Align "power of life" with belonging and impact.

Links: [[Mind]] for values (connection, creativity, curiosity); daily rituals from [[mind_problems]]; [[Goals]] for family/meaning ties.

## Actionable Next Steps
- **Short-Term**: Resume [[Morning Pages]] or 5-min journaling to "exorcise thought demons." Track triggers in a simple table:
  
  | Trigger | Feeling | Ritual Tried | Outcome |
  |---------|---------|--------------|---------|
  | Unstructured day | Overwhelm | Meditation | Partial relief |
  | Family milestone | Guilt | Quality time | Grounding |

- **Long-Term**: Build a "framework" note linking ideas (e.g., PMP, fermenting) to principles. Explore therapy for neuro-divergence/trauma; revisit [[Sleep]] hygiene to break vicious cycles.
